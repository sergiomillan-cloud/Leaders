Leaders Build It Right

Lancer Leaders of People Training Program

Duration: 6 Modules | Session Length: 1.5 hours (includes lunch)

Program Objective

Equip leaders with the knowledge, judgment, and tools to manage people effectively, ensure compliance, and foster a high-performance work environment.

Module 1: Supervisor Role and Compliance Framework

Objective: Understand supervisor responsibilities and compliance requirements.

  • Exempt vs. Non-exempt employees
  • Work time and labor compliance
  • Documentation and consistency
  • Myths vs. realities in supervision

Module 2: Timesheets and Attendance

Objective: Ensure accurate and compliant timekeeping.

  • Overtime rules and approvals
  • Time corrections and validation
  • Audit readiness and evidence
  • Standard workflow
  • Attendance issues

Module 3: Leaves of Absence (LOA)

Objective: Understand leave policies and compliance.

  • LOA triggers
  • FMLA basics
  • ADA accommodations
  • Confidentiality and HR coordination

Module 4: Policies & Procedures

Objective: Promote a safe and compliant workplace.

  • Safety and PPE
  • Code of Ethics and Anti-Harassment
  • Device usage rules
  • Consistency across shifts

Module 5: Disciplinary Actions & Performance Improvement

Objective: Apply fair discipline practices.

  • Performance vs. conduct
  • Progressive discipline
  • Investigation steps
  • PIP structure

Module 6: Real-Time Coaching

Objective: Enhance coaching skills.

  • SBI Model
  • GROW Model
  • 1:1 coaching
  • Positive reinforcement
